• If the person has come into contact with poison on their skin. rinse the affected area with water for at least 15 minutes
  • If the person has ingested poison, do not induce vomiting or give them anything to eat or drink, including medication, unless instructed to do so by medical professionals.
  • If the person is unconscious but breathing, place them in the recovery position on their side with their head supported
  • Provide as much information about the poison as possible to emergency responders, including the type of poison
